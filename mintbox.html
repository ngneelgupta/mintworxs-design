About MintBox

With the evolving technologies every customer has the need of a flexible platform which can bring value to their business, Our Product is developed keeping in mind the current business problems and giving one step custom solution with MintBox. MintBox is an AI/ML based CMS platform to launch any kind of website with ease. This platform can auto apply tags, interlink pages, capture the data from any kind of document, can perform on page SEO optimisation and do quick integrations.

Social Association Experience

This module focuses on improving the social interaction experience of your business, MintBox provides various tools and features for attaining an impactful social presence.

Some of eye catching features will be:

1

Blogs — MintBox includes a full featured blogging platform built for easy content authoring and sharing. The newest features include friendly URL creation, estimated reading times and inline videos.

2

Mint Hawk — Mint Hawk uses AI techniques to learn what kind of content does well on your site. By keeping track of the number of upvotes and downvotes each of your posts gets on social media, it can automatically eliminate content that would otherwise crowd your feeds, thereby boosting your site's performance.

3

Mint Spam checker — Mint spam checker uses machine learning technologies to check comments on your application, social site posts and contact form submissions against the global database of spam to prevent your site from publishing malicious content.

  • You can review the comment spam it catches on your blog’s “Comments” admin screen.
  • Filters out the ones that look like spam.
  • Maintain status history of comments to see which comments were caught or cleared by Mint spam checker and which were spammed or unspammed.
  • Save disk space and speed up your site.
4

Auto image resizing and size recommendation engine - Resize, crop and make color adjustments to images uploaded from within applications that use an item selector. The image editor is customizable so developers can create and deploy tools that modify images.

5

Mint Survey - Allows users and administrators to create multiple choice polls that keep track of votes and display results on the page.

6

Social Networking — Add social networking features such as an activity wall, friends list, @ mentions, activity tracker and social activity. Easily bookmark URLs in the platform and share them to a group of users.
